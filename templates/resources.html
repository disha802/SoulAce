Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)

CBT helps identify and reframe negative thought patterns into more balanced perspectives. Try challenging unhelpful thoughts with evidence-based reasoning.

×

Mindful Breathing

Slow down with intentional breaths. Inhale for 4 counts, hold for 4, exhale for 6. This helps ground you in the present and reduce stress.

×

Journaling

Writing your thoughts and emotions on paper can provide clarity, release stress, and track your growth over time.

×

Meditation

Spend a few minutes each day focusing on your breath or a calming visualization. Meditation can lower anxiety and improve emotional resilience.
