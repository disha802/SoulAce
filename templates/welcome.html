Frequently Asked Questions

Your concerns matter to us. Here are answers to common questions.

Will my family or community know I'm using this platform?

+
Absolutely not. We understand the importance of privacy in our culture. Your identity is completely anonymous - no real names, phone numbers, or identifiable information is required. All sessions are confidential and your data is protected with end-to-end encryption. Your mental health journey remains private.

How does the AI understand our cultural context and emotions?

+
Our AI is trained with cultural sensitivity in mind, understanding the unique emotional expressions and family dynamics common in diverse communities. It responds in multiple languages, recognizing cultural phrases and providing coping strategies that align with various values while maintaining modern therapeutic approaches.

Are the therapists familiar with our cultural background?

+
Yes! Our licensed therapists are specifically chosen for their understanding of diverse cultural backgrounds, family structures, and mental health perspectives. They specialize in culturally competent care for various issues while respecting different religious and cultural values.

How much does it cost to use SoulAce?

+
We offer a freemium model with basic features available at no cost, including AI chat support and community forums. Premium features like one-on-one therapy sessions have affordable pricing options with flexible payment plans. We believe mental health support should be accessible to everyone.

Is SoulAce suitable for severe mental health conditions?

+
While SoulAce provides excellent support for anxiety, depression, stress, and everyday mental health challenges, we always recommend consulting with healthcare professionals for severe conditions. Our platform includes crisis support features and can help connect you with appropriate emergency resources when needed.
