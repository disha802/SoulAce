Frequently Asked Questions

Your concerns matter to us. Here are answers to common questions.

Will my family or community know I'm using this platform?

+
Absolutely not. We understand the importance of privacy in our culture. Your identity is completely anonymous - no real names, phone numbers, or identifiable information is required. All sessions are confidential and your data is protected with end-to-end encryption. Your mental health journey remains private.

How does the AI understand our cultural context and emotions?

+
Our AI is trained with cultural sensitivity in mind, understanding the unique emotional expressions and family dynamics common in South Asian communities. It responds in Hindi, Urdu, and English, recognizing cultural phrases and providing coping strategies that align with our values while maintaining modern therapeutic approaches.

Are the therapists familiar with our cultural background?

+
Yes! Our licensed therapists are specifically chosen for their understanding of South Asian culture, family structures, and mental health stigmas. They specialize in culturally competent care for issues like family pressure, arranged marriages, career expectations, and intergenerational trauma while respecting religious and cultural values.
